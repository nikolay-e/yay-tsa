The library starts EMPTY. To add music, mount it and trigger a scan:
    # 1. Make your library available to the backend (preferred: a pre-created PVC):
    #    --set yay-tsa-v2.backend.media.enabled=true \
    #    --set yay-tsa-v2.backend.media.existingClaim=<your-pvc>   (or .hostPath on single-node)
    # 2. Trigger a rescan (admin token from login):
    #    curl -X POST http://localhost:8080/api/Admin/Library/Rescan -H "Authorization: Bearer <token>"
